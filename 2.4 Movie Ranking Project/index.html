My Best 3 Moives

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A wrongly convicted banker forms a deep friendship with a fellow inmate and fights to keep hope alive in the brutal Shawshank prison. A powerful story of resilience and redemption.

Inception (2010)

A skilled thief enters people’s dreams to steal secrets but must now attempt the impossible — planting an idea into someone’s mind. A mind-bending film by Christopher Nolan.

The Dark Knight (2008)

Batman faces his toughest enemy yet — the Joker, a criminal mastermind who wants to plunge Gotham into chaos. A gripping exploration of justice, morality, and sacrifice.
